what web-designer do?

Web design identifies the goals of a website or webpage and promotes accessibility for all potential user. This process involves organizing content and images acorss a series of pages, integrating applications and other interactive elements.

The professional who perform this process are called web designers, and their job includes the following duties:

  • selecting easy-to-read fonts
  • Choosing attractive color schemes that also enable easy-to-read fonts
  • Implementing a brand's dentity into the colors, fonts and layout
  • Creating a map of the website's structure to ensure intuitive navigation
  • Using coding language, such as HTML and CSS, to creat layouts and to style pages
  • Making optimized versions of websites and pages both for desktop and mobile viewings
